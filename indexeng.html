YTY Studio

Youth Teaching Youth

Founded by television anchors and professional event master of ceremonies, YTY Studio provides structured public-speaking programs in Hong Kong, Mainland and Taiwan for diverse settings ranging from government entities to corporate companies. Our clients include HKTDC, HK Towngas, AIA Insurance and more. To promote the important of public speaking, we also co-organized speaking contest with Sun-City group. ​
YTY Studio is not only the initials of the founders, but it also means Youth teaching Youth. Speaking is an important aspect in life. We require it to express ourselves, to communicate with others, to present to clients, etc. With the right training and practices, we believe everyone is able to express themselves confidently. Our goal is to improve your self-esteem with your speech and hence achieve better in your life.

Corporate Training

Each company is unique and special in their own way, and one’s need is often different from the others. Therefore, we tailor-made speaking-related training for conglomerates, corporations and even SMEs based on the goals they hope to achieve and that usually involves the improvement of charisma, presentation skills, sales and communication, and dealing with complaints.


School/NGOs Sharing

Hosting-experience-sharing and training are often organized for primary and secondary schools and universities, in where they can know more about television and event industry and expand their career choices.




MC Training

Regularly, we organize training courses for students, job-seekers, employees and even executives who are interested in being a master of ceremonies and improving their presentation and communication skills. At clsss, instructors will teach theories and allow practices with various event scenarios. We care for each student and wish to provide extra attention for everyone of them; therefore, all courses are limited to a small group. And for those who would like to be MCs in the future, we will always try our best to allow practices in real events.

• Based on the level of the courses, we provide different kinds & levels of materials to the students.
• During the class, instructors will provide theories and allow practices with expected & unexpected event scenarios.
• Students with potential will be added to our MC list, from where we choose our MC for their events.
• All courses are limited to a small group to allow practices for each student.



Mc Services

Master of Ceremonies plays such an important factor in each event. A MCs can ruin the event but can also turn it around. To ensure each client find their perfect MCs for their event, we have a range of professional MCs who are fluent in Cantonese, English and Mandarin, and some can even speak Japanese, Korean and Burmese in cases of special guests in the events. All of our MCs are well-trained and experienced and are capable of handling different kinds of events and situation.
